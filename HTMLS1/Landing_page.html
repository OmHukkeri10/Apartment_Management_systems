Rules and Regulations

  • Curfew: Be home before 11:00 PM
  • Vehicle Limit: Max 1 four-wheeler and 2 two-wheelers per flat.
  • Parking: Park only in designated spots; unauthorized parking may result in fines.
  • Pets: Pets allowed with approval; must be kept on a leash and cleaned up after.
  • Balcony Usage: No storage or hanging clothes on balconies.
  • Smoking: No smoking in common areas.

    • History

    Akshaya Garden Apartment was built in the year 2004 by the owner, Sagar S. It is located in Vidyanagar, Hubli. The apartment com+plex consists of 10 blocks, named Block A to Block J, with each block containing 10 flats. The flats are either owned by the residents or rented out to tenants, offering a diverse living community. Akshaya Garden has since become a well-established residential area, known for its peaceful environment and convenient location.
